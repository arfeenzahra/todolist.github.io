Plan For Ramzan

First 10 Days

ToDoList
  • Recite the Qur'an(1-10)
  • Daily Dikr,morning,evening,after prayer
  • Engage in all Du'a of first ashra
  • Dua:
    رَبِّ اغْفِرْ وَارْحَمْ وَأَنْتَ خَيْرُ الرَّاحِمِينَ
    "O My Lord forgives and has Mercy and you are the best of Merciful "
  • Reflect on and Improve Yourself
  • pray tahajjud every night
  • Daily surah Rehman
  • Daily only one hour(3pm-4pm) for university work

Next 10 Days

ToDoList
  • Recite the Qur'an(11-20)
  • Daily Dikr,morning,evening,after prayer
  • Engage in all Du'a of second ashra
  • Dua:
    اَسْتَغْفِرُ اللہَ رَبِّی مِنْ کُلِّ زَنْبٍ وَّ اَتُوْبُ اِلَیْہِ
    "seek forgiveness from Allah for all my sins and turn to Him"
  • Give in Charity
  • pray tahajjud every night
  • Daily surah Yaseen
  • Daily only one hour(3pm-4pm) for university work

Last 10 Days

ToDoList
  • Recite the Qur'an(21-30)
  • Daily Dikr,morning,evening,after prayer
  • Engage in all Du'a of third ashra
  • Dua:
    اَللَّهُمَّ أَجِرْنِي مِنَ النَّارِ
